Ginger Dressing

This classic taste of Asian Hibachi dressing made easy to recreate in your own home

Just buy the following ingredients at your local grocery store or produce shop and enjoy

Let's get started

Information

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 20-24 (depenging on size of salads)

Ingredients

  • Granulated Sugar
  • Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Peanut Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Lemons or Lemon Juice
  • Water
  • Ketchup
  • Celery
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Vidalia/Sweet Onion

Instructions

Using a Blender or Food Processor do the following:

  1. Place 34g of sugar, 9g of sea salt, and 2g of black pepper in small dish and fully incorporate together with a fork; set aside
  2. Pour 235g rice vinegar, 314g peanut oil, 64g soy sauce, 26g lemon juice, 12g water and 155g ketchup into blender or food processor
  3. Add sugar, salt and pepper mix into blender
  4. Blend ingredients in blender together until mixture appears uniform
  5. Roughly chop 3.9oz celery, peel and roughly chop 3oz of ginger, roughly chop 7.5oz sweet onions; place all in blender
  6. Peel and smash 3 medium sized cloves of garlic and add to blender
  7. Blend mixture for 2-3minutes or until mixture appears uniform
  8. Transfer mixture to bottles
  9. Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving
  10. Enjoy!
